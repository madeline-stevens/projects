About

Maddy Stevens

I am an independent UX Researcher and Web Developer with a strong focus on Accessibility. I audit websites and apps to ensure they are ADA (Americans with Disabilities) and WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) compliant. I'm passionate about this because I used to teach folks who are blind/low vision how to use Voiceover(Apple’s screen reader technology). My goal is to make all online spaces as accessible as possible for as many people as possible.
