About

Professionally:

My goal has always been to make all online spaces as accessible as possible for as many people as possible.

When I worked at Apple Retail, where I specialized in teaching one-on-one and group workshops to folks with disabilities, I met a woman who went to bed sighted and woke up blind. I am constantly thinking about her when I’m working on this practice.

I also worked with regulars and it was as frustrating for them as it was for me when we’d come across an inaccessible website or app. So from there I just wanted to dig my heels into these problems. I just wanted to get to work.

Hello hello! My name is Maddy and I am an independent UX Researcher and Web Developer with a focus on Accessibility.

I audit websites and apps to ensure they are ADA (Americans with Disabilities) and WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) compliant.
